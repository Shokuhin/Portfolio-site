Key VIA Projects

KidsREAD 2024

Volunteering to be a part of KidsREAD, I led storytellings to large groups of students of Gan Eng Seng Primary School, and taught them basic concepts of linguistics and arithmetic in small groups. I love children and this project taught me how to engage them!!

Volunteer at ASEAN Pavilion Booth at World Robotics Olympiad 2025

As a Volunteer, I worked with the rest of the team to attract visitors and teach what ASEAN is about to visitors from around the globe, from Cameroon to South Korea and so many more amazing countries. I am very grateful to have been able to interact with so many different people!

Youth STEM Empowerment Programme 2024

Together with the team, we met directly with the elderly to empathise with their struggles and designed a functional smart pill dispenser(PillPal) to minimise life-threatening patient medication errors. In the process, I learned how real and scary the troubles seniors face are, deepening my respect for them!
